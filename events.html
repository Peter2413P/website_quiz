How to Participate

Joining our events is easy! Here's what you need to know:

  • Registration: Most events require pre-registration through our website
  • Teams: Usually 2-4 members per team (varies by event)
  • Fees: Some events have nominal participation fees to cover costs
  • Preparation: No special preparation needed - come as you are!
  • Prizes: Winners receive trophies, certificates, and sometimes cash prizes
